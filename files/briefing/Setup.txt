Your team leader has the ability to select your team's starting location near your target during the briefing phase.
<br /> <br />
As a leader, ALT-click the map inside the <marker name = 'mrk_exampleOuter_%1'>outer limit</marker>, but outside of the <marker name = 'mrk_exampleInner_%1'>inner limit</marker>, and on land, to place or move your setup marker. You will start at that location.
