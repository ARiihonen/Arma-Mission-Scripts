After the civil war, Altis has been under a long occupation by foreign forces, initially intended to be an interim operation until control could be left to the local security apparatus.
<br /> <br />
The efforts have been in vain, and as instability has grown and a hopeful government after another has fallen, the people have grown restless.
<br /> <br />
With weapons acquired through a thriving black market supported by the ineffectiveness of the government and the fatigue of the occupying forces, a massive uprising has now broken out. The island has been driven into chaos, and large amounts of armed insurgents are fighting anyone they consider to be responsible for the state of the island.
